Join the FlipDeck Mission

FlipDeck is inspired by the now discontinued TinyCards app. Our mission is to create a flashcard app for users of all ages (middle school and up) to make short quizzes on any topic. Study your own deck of cards, then review them with true/false and multiple choice questions.

Why Join Us?

  • Be part of building something meaningful
  • Contribute ideas and features
  • Get early access to the app
  • Join a community of learners and creators
