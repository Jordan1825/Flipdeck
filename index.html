Welcome to FlipDeck

FlipDeck, Inspired by the now discontinued TinyCards app, FlipDeck's mission is for users of a young age (middle school and up) to make short quizzes of any topic. It begins with studying your own deck of cards, and then review them all with true/false, and multiple choice questions

BENEFITS

  • Create flashcards quickly using text or images
  • Friendly smile/frown response for feedback to make learning less stressful
  • Simple enough for middle-schoolers but powerful enough for adults
